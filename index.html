Student Life & Campus Infrastructure

Classrooms

Our college boasts state-of-the-art classrooms designed to foster a conducive learning environment. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids, including digital projectors, smart boards, and high-speed internet access.

Canteen

Our canteen serves a wide variety of hygienic and nutritious meals, catering to the diverse tastes of our students and staff. From quick snacks to full meals, the canteen offers something for everyone at affordable prices. The spacious dining area provides a comfortable space for students to relax and socialize during breaks, with a focus on cleanliness and quality.

Library

The college library is a hub of knowledge, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources across various engineering fields. Students have access to a vast array of e-books, research papers, and online databases, enabling them to stay updated with the latest advancements in technology. The library provides a quiet and peaceful environment for study and research, with dedicated reading areas and computer stations available for use.

Bus Facilities

To ensure hassle-free commuting, the college provides a fleet of well-maintained buses that cover major routes across the city and nearby areas. The buses are equipped with comfortable seating and safety features, ensuring a secure and convenient mode of transportation for students and staff. The bus service operates on a punctual schedule, ensuring timely arrival and departure from the college.
